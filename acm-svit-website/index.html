About ACM

The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) Student Chapter at SVIT is a dynamic community of tech enthusiasts, developers, and innovators. We are dedicated to advancing computing as a science and a profession.

Our goal is to foster a culture of continuous learning and collaboration. Whether you are a beginner writing your first line of code, or an experienced developer building complex systems, ACM SVIT is the place where you can grow, network, and turn your ideas into reality.

Empowering Students

Building a robust developer ecosystem on campus through peer-to-peer learning and hands-on experience.

Key Activities

Hackathons

Intense, 24-48 hour coding marathons where students collaborate to build creative solutions for real-world problems.

Workshops

Hands-on sessions on the latest technologies including Web Development, AI/ML, Cloud Computing, and App Dev.

Coding Competitions

Regular DSA and competitive programming contests to sharpen algorithmic thinking and problem-solving skills.
